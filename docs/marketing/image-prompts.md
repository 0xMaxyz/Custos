# Custos — Image Generation Prompts (Google Imagen / Gemini)

Prompts for generating marketing & promotional images with Google's image models
(Imagen 4 in the Gemini app / AI Studio / "Nano Banana"). Tuned to the Custos brand.

## Brand cheat-sheet (keep consistent across all images)
- **Name & meaning:** Custos — Latin for *guardian / keeper* (echoes *custody*).
- **Idea to convey:** an **AI guardian** that earns safe real-yield on tokenized US
  Treasuries and **automatically defends** your dollars on-chain, *verifiably*.
- **Mark:** a **shield with a check** (lucide `shield-check`).
- **Accent color:** violet **`#7C3AED`** (light) / **`#8B5CF6`** (dark).
- **Neutrals:** near-black slate `#0B1020`–`#0F172A`, off-white `#F8FAFC`, slate grays.
- **Tone:** calm, professional, restrained, trustworthy. Generous whitespace.
  *Not* flashy crypto-bro neon. Think fintech/security, not casino.
- **Network:** Mantle. **Assets:** USDC, USDY (tokenized Treasuries), AUSD.
- **Tagline options:** "*The guardian that proves every move.*" /
  "*Real yield. Watched. Defended. On-chain.*"

## Tips for Google's models
- Write natural-language scene descriptions, not keyword soup. State **subject,
  composition, style, lighting, mood, color palette, and aspect ratio**.
- **Avoid baked-in text/logos** — image models garble words. Generate clean imagery
  with negative space and add the wordmark/copy in Canva/Figma afterward. Where a
  prompt does request a word, keep it to one short token and expect to redo it.
- Add aspect ratio in the Gemini/AI Studio settings (1:1, 16:9, 9:16, 4:5) and/or
  state it in the prompt.
- Re-run 3–4 times and pick; iterate by editing one variable at a time.

### Quick negative-prompt guidance (if your tool supports it)
Avoid: garbled text, fake logos, neon casino vibes, cluttered backgrounds, busy crypto
clichés (lambos, bulls/bears, gold coins everywhere), stocky hands shaking, watermarks,
distorted faces.
